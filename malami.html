MALAMI RECEIVES SEVERAL AWARDS, COMMENDATIONS
No fewer than Eighty organizations and corporate bodies have conferred award of honour for patriotic service, humanitarian engagements and nation building to the Attorney General of the Federation and Minister of Justice, Abubakar Malami, SAN in the year 2021.
 A statistical breakdown, on the development, from the office of the Attorney General of the Federation and Minister of Justice indicated that from January to March, 2021 fifteen organizations sent in their notification for the awards and honour.
While 26 organizations wrote between April to June, 18 and twenty organizations wrote letters of notification for the award of honour during third and fourth quarters of 2021 respectively.
The organizations comprised of national and international institutions from Governmental, non-governmental organizations, anti-corruption and forensic investigation bodies, professional associations, student unions, media-based groups and traditional institutions.
